San Diego, California

Data

San Diego is known for its beaches, parks, and warm climate. It's a major city in California, located on the coast of the Pacific Ocean.

Population: 1.4 million (as of 2023)

Area: 372.4 square miles

Major Attractions: Balboa Park, San Diego Zoo, SeaWorld San Diego, Old Town San Diego State Historic Park.

Weather ⛅

San Diego enjoys a mild Mediterranean climate with warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters.

Average High Temperature: 70°F (21°C)

Average Low Temperature: 57°F (14°C)

Annual Precipitation: 10 inches (254 mm)
